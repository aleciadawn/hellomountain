About the Band
Hello, Mountain is a Denver, Colorado-based indie rock trio originally formed in 2015 by Stephen Pamas (Vocals, Guitar), Jack Falk (Bass), and Patrick Smith (Drums, Synth, Backing Vocals). With a wide range of influences, the band has crafted a unique sound centered around singer-songwriter Stephen Pamas. The band has released three lengthy EP’s with a full length record in the works.
